Jason Allan's Headline Gig Is Just Around The Corner And He Has Revealed All Four Special Guests

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, March 25, 2024

Way back in November, we were excited to write about Jason Allan announcing his first headline show. Now, we’re only 23 days away from the big event, which will be taking place at Liverpool’s Studio 2. Four special guests have been announced in recent weeks, they are Franklin Lake, Angel Gold, Bailey Nelsen, and Robbie White.

The 10 February 2018 is set to be an exciting date with many talented acts taking to the stage. Jason Allan always brings a great stage presence with original songs and well-known covers. He has been gigging since the age of 17; now at 20, his musical career has taken steps in the right direction, with a headline show, a debut single on its way titled “If I Could”, along with an upcoming accompanying music video.

Recently, he has been working with Lana Del Rey’s producer, Tim Larcombe. Not only that, he has also been songwriting with rising band Kovic. He has built up a loyal fan-base who will certainly be following his every musical step.

The four special guests weren’t his only announcements when it comes to this headline show. He also revealed Jason Allan merchandise hoodie that he modelled in a video posted on Twitter. For those of you who can’t make his show, the hoodie will be available on his website the day after, 11 February 2018.

https://twitter.com/JasonAllanUK/status/953331216702496769

Tickets for the show are available now. The gig is certainly one you won’t want to miss. Apart from Jason Allan, there will be four incredible special guests. Franklin Lake have thousands of Spotify streams, their most popular single being “Glad We Met” which has 11,000 streams. Angel Gold was voted as Jason Allan’s special guest through Facebook, she released her debut single “Your Love Is My Drug” in October.

Also acting as a special guest is Bailey Nelsen who is an 18-year-old singer who has released cover videos on Instagram; and, then there’s Robbie White, who has been gaining fans and followers by gigging and has recently announced his own headline show.

As previously stated, tickets are available on his official website. “General Admission” tickets are just £10.00 each plus a £1.35 fee. You can also get “General Admission + Parent/Guardian” tickets which are an additional £5. Get your tickets now, while you still can. The show will take place at Studio 2 in Liverpool on 10 February 2018 at 1 pm.
