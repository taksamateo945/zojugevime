Jay Z Allegedly Wanted To Intervene Between Lil Boosie And Dwyane Wade Regarding Comments Made About

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, March 26, 2024

Before he was the king of Instagram Live, Lil Boosie came under fire for comments he made about Dwyane Wade’s trans daughter. The rapper recently revealed that he was told Jay Z wanted to intervene.

If you haven’t been up to date with the aforementioned situation, Dwyane revealed to the world that his daughter has asked to be called Zaya Wade. Not only was he fully supportive of the change, but he also has been taking the time to educate himself on raising a trans child.

After the announcement was made, Boosie took to Instagram Live where he went on a transphobic tirade asking the athlete not to the ‘cut the boy’s d*** off.’

Opinions were split when it came to the public’s reaction. While some claim that the musician was just saying how he felt others said that talking about people’s children is off-limits.

Even Boosie’s own mother called to reprimand him about his actions.

In a recent Breakfast Club interview, Boosie claims that Jay Z wanted to set up a phone call between him and the former Heat player so he could apologize.

‘They say Jay-Z was gonna get him on the phone and all that shit. I need to apologize to save this and save that,’ he explained to Charlamagne Tha God.

However, the phone call never transpired because Boosie is standing by what he said.

‘Tell Jay-Z I don’t wanna talk. I’m not apologizing for s***. I don’t give a f*** if Jesus calling to get me to apologize. I said what I said, man. I felt that was right.’

Boosie was there to promote his new reality show titled Boosie Badazz that is allegedly coming to the FUBU network after some of the rapper’s antics went viral.
