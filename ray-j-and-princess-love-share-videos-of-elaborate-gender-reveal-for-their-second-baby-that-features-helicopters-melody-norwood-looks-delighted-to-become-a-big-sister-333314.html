Ray J And Princess Love Share Videos Of Elaborate Gender Reveal For Their Second Baby That Features

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 19, 2024

Ray J and Princess Love already have one child, (cutie pie Melody Norwood), and recent reports indicate that another young one was on the way.

And while the two have been trying to keep the situation under wraps and have not been sharing too many details about their current pregnancy, sources seem to have confirmed the gender of the new child.

According to reports, it is going to be a boy — making the couple the proud parents of the classic brother/sister duo.

Their young girl, Melody, is already one year old and has been growing up fast, and it looks like the two are more than ready to welcome another child into their family.

It is not clear what plans they might have for their new boy — if anything specific — but the two have made it clear that they know how to be good, responsible parents, and they are going to keep pushing in the same direction.

The boy is reportedly set to arrive in January, although this has not been confirmed yet.
