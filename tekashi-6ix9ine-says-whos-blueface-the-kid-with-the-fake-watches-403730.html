Tekashi 6ix9ine Says Whos Blueface? The Kid With The Fake Watches?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, February 25, 2024

Tekashi 6ix9ine recently did an Instagram Live session with his friend, DJ Akademiks. Akademiks and Tekashi 6ix9ine were on Instagram for approximately one hour for a session in which they spoke about the music industry and also chatted with fans.

The pair of men also called out other rappers in the industry, in a conversation that will likely make a lot of people mad. Regardless of the eventual reaction, Akademiks and Daniel Hernandez will always talk about what’s on their minds.

At one point in their conversation, Tekashi 6ix9ine joked that if he sold more than Lil’ Uzi Vert this year, Akademiks had to get rid of his Instagram account. They also talked about the rappers out there who have been trying to do songs with him but publicly treating him like a pariah.

Furthermore, Akademiks asked Tekashi 6ix9ine about other rappers in the scene and one-hit wonders. Names such as Blueface, BlocBoy JB, and YK Osiris all came up. When Akademiks brought up Blueface, Tekashi 6ix9one asked if he was the kid with all the “fake watches.”

Of course, Tekashi 6ix9ine has garnered a reputation for himself in which people in the industry call him out for snitching on his former associates in the Nine Trey Gangsta Bloods. 6ix9ine says that he made his decision in court that day and he has never looked back since.

It’s clear that Tekashi 6ix9ine remains in high spirits following his release to home confinement earlier this year. The topic of his mockery, Blueface, has faced his own setbacks, including when he uploaded a video on his social media in which one of his baby mommas was damaging his house in front of their child.
