Arianne Hartono Dating Status, Parents &amp; Family Background

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, March 28, 2024

Arianne Hartono’s love for tennis came mostly from the maternal side of her family. She is now enjoying her professional career with numerous doubles and singles titles in her name.

Arianne Hartono is dating a tennis coach boyfriend. Get to know her love life, parents, family background, ethnicity, career, and interesting facts here.

Arianne Hartono is a Dutch tennis player who has a WTA doubles ranking of world no. 145. She has a career-high doubles ranking of no. 123 achieved on 11 July 2022.

Meet Arianne Hartono’s Boyfriend

Hartono is now in a relationship with her boyfriend Jason Ontog.

Jason is also a tennis player who is inactive now. He played collegiate at the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga and was one of the most decorated student-athletes in the storied history of the college.


Moreover, he was a women’s tennis coach at the University of Mississippi. The duo began dating when she was attending college.

The couple is enjoying their love life despite the huge age gap between them.

Arianne Hartono Family & Ethnicity

Arianne was born to parents of diverse ethnic backgrounds. She was born on 21 April 1996 in Meppel, Netherlands.

The tennis player’s father is Lieke Hartono and her mother is Okki Hartono. Both her parents hail from Indonesia and came to the Netherlands in the 1990s. Her love for tennis comes mostly from her maternal side as her uncle Deddy Tedjamukti and aunt Lukky Tedjamukti were professional tennis players from Indonesia.


Arianne has an elder brother named Adriaan Hartono who is a pharmacist by profession. She was introduced to tennis by her brother who also played tennis in his early days.

Her Career Journey

Hartono played her first competitive match at age 16 on the ITF World Tennis Tour in the Netherlands in 2012. She went to the University of Mississippi to play collegiate tennis from 2014 to 2018.

In her senior year, she won the 2018 NCAA Division I Women’s Tennis Championship. Eventually, she became the first player to win the championship from her country.


Arianne made her Grand Slam debut at the 2022 Australian Open. As of now, she has won two singles and fifteen doubles titles on the ITF Circuit. She recently competed at her second Australian Open in 2023.

Some Interesting Facts About Arianne Hartono

  • Arianne is multilingual and can speak Indonesian, Dutch, English, and Chinese languages.
  • She also competed in swimming in her early days.
  • Her boyfriend was inducted into the 2010 Chattanooga Tennis Hall of Fame.
  • Her cousin Nadia Ravita played collegiate tennis at the University of Kentucky.
