Top 10 Instafamous Dogs to Follow That Pull at Your Heart Strings

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 11, 2024

@Tunameltsmyheart

The underdog with the overbite…

The underdog with the overbite, Tuna the Chiweenie has a recessed jaw, a floppy neck and over a million followers on Instagram. The dog has a travel blog, Traveling Tuna, and his own merchandise line. He uses his social media soapbox to preach on the joys of napping and unconventional beauty.

@Toastmeetsworld

Rescued from a puppy mill by Josh “The Fat Jew” Ostrovsky and publicist Katie Sturino…

Rescued from a puppy mill by Josh “The Fat Jew” Ostrovsky and publicist Katie Sturino, Toast needed some serious dental work. The result? A tongue that won’t stay in her mouth, giving her an idgaf vibe and rating her as one of the cutest dogs on Instagram. Going from rags to riches gives Toast the license to be fancy af; she had an A-lister doggy wedding to husband Finn the Chihuahua – Dachshund mix and her own book on how to summer in the Hamptons. She does photo shoots, models top of the line sunglasses and loves tacos.

@BuddyBoowaggytails

Boo, the “World’s Cutest Dog”…

Boo, the “World’s Cutest Dog” has made six figures off of just being adorable. The pup’s got a huge following, a merchandise line, two book deals and even a movie. He makes up one-third of the “Blonde Mafia” or “B3” along with his two siblings, Buddy and Sir Blueberry. Plus, he’s got friends in high places – say hi to Seth Rogen and Ke$ha for us, Boo!

@itsdougthepug

Doug the Pug, self-proclaimed King of Pop Culture and master of disguise…

Doug the Pug, self-proclaimed King of Pop Culture and master of disguise, rose to fame with the help of PR guru mom, Kendall Mosier. He loves pepperoni pizza, snuggling in blankets and doughnut-shaped pillows. Doug has a book on the New York Times bestseller list and over 2 million Instagram followers. The young pug’s popularity surpasses the social media world; offline, he’s friends with the likes of Justin Bieber and Ed Sheeran. Doug’s successes aren’t all online, he’s also made a couple bucks for his human parents.

@Marniethedog

Marnie the Shih Tzu went from poor to prosperous after Shirley Braha adopted the senior from a shelter…

Marnie the Shih Tzu went from poor to prosperous after Shirley Braha adopted the senior from a shelter. After some TLC Marnie, previously known as Stinky, was lookin’ fresh to death and began amassing online followers. Her iconic extra long tongue and permanent head tilt have given her a notoriety for the cutest dog on the net. Marnie’s also became the poster pup for elder dog adoption. The fluffy little babe routinely poses with celebrities, does paid appearances at events, the occasional commercial and has her own merchandise line.
Not bad for an old gal.

@Manny_the_frenchie

From runt to top dog: Mannie the French Bulldog was sold to his parents for a discount price…

From runt to top dog: Mannie the French Bulldog was sold to his parents for a discount price ‘cause no one else would take him. His humans started posting pictures of him sleeping in the sink at home and before they knew it, he was an Instagram star with clothing line offers. American Apparel originally started printing t-shirts with Mannie’s face on them and now you can get anything from pins and pillows to baby onesies. Mannie’s celeb status has brought in enough off of his merch line and endorsements to allow mom, Amber Chavez, to quit her job and become his manager full time.

@Maymothedog

Maymo the Lemon Beagle has one of the most entertaining IG accounts by far…

Maymo the Lemon Beagle has one of the most entertaining IG accounts by far. The dog is a self proclaimed “master of disguise & purveyor of skullduggery”. His human’s have over a hundred funny YouTube videos featuring Maymo and his sister Penny going on bizarre adventures, like trips in a grocery cart across town to grab some burgers.

@Samsonthedood

Samson the Goldendoodle is currently the hottest socialite in Brooklyn…

Samson the Goldendoodle is currently the hottest socialite in Brooklyn. Like any self-respecting New York hipster, he hangs out on rooftop bars or at posh parties sponsored by companies like Coach. He enjoys brunch with his doggie pals, photoshoots by the Brooklyn bridge and lounging in his plush robe. Oh, and hanging out with his bff Bea, the teddy bear.

@harlowandsage

Harlow, a perpetually shocked Weimaraner…

Harlow, a perpetually shocked Weimaraner lives with her two sassy wiener dog sisters, Indiana Thunderbolt and Reese Lightning. Workin’ dogs who buy their own bones, the trio have their own merchandise line, two books and over a million IG followers. They help their humans with the bills by doing an occasional sponsored post.

@Crusoe_dachshund

Crusoe the celebrity wiener dog has been taking his fame to new levels…

Crusoe the celebrity wiener dog has been taking his fame to new levels. He’s got a hilarious award winning dog blog and has been featured on more talk shows than you could shake a stick at. He’s got a couple commercials under his belt and about a hundred hilarious dog videos. His many alter-egos include Dog Ross the artist, Chef Crusoe, Batdog and our personal favourite, the ‘long paw of the law’ Officer Crusoe. He’s got calendars, books and a variety of swag available.
