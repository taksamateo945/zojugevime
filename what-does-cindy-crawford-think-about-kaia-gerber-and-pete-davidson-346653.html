What Does Cindy Crawford Think About Kaia Gerber And Pete Davidson?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 5, 2024

Watching your child date someone you don’t particularly care for can be a tricky situation. Now that Cindy Crawford’s look-alike, model daughter Kaia Gerber has been spending a great deal of time with Saturday Night Live (SNL) cast member Pete Davidson, people are asking what Cindy really thinks about the relationship. At 25-years-old, Pete Davidson isn’t that much older than Kaia who just turned 18-years-old in September, but for some parents, they might feel that there is a bit of an age difference.

Cindy hasn’t spoken directly about Kaia and Pete dating, but Us Weekly exclusively quotes a source who says they have inside information regarding the situation.

Cindy Crawford is married to Rande Gerber and the couple has two children: twenty-year-old son Presley who is also a model and Kaia Gerber.

According to Us Weekly, Cindy is trying to stay out of it and not interfering with Kaia’s happiness, while at the same time trying to steer Kaia into focusing on herself and her future.

Cindy Crawford has experienced many of the situations that Kaia will find herself in. As one of the first supermodels, Cindy knows the modeling industry, the lifestyle, the parties and what it means to be a celebrity.  Cindy and Kaia spoke about many of the ways the industry is both similar and different in a Vogue interview.

According to Us Weekly, the source stated the following to explain how Cindy feels about the relationship.

“Cindy’s supportive of Kaia and wants her to be happy. Right now she’s happy with Pete, so Cindy’s trying not to get involved.”
