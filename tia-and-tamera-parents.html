Tia and Tamera Parents: Timothy and Darlene Mowry

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Who are Tia and Tamera Parents? In this article we reveal the biological parents of the twin sisters.

During the 1990s, there is a high likelihood that you would have come across the names Tia and Tamera Mowry, known for their significant presence in the entertainment industry.

These two individuals are identical twins who achieved fame through their prominent roles in the popular 90s sitcom titled Sister, Sister.

The catalyst behind their entry into the world of show business can be attributed to their parents, who played a pivotal role in shaping their careers.

Tia and Tamera Mowry have become iconic figures in Hollywood, recognized as one of the most well-known twin pairs.

Their acting prowess has led to successful and enduring careers that have spanned over three decades.

However, it is important to note that the narrative of their triumph would be incomplete without acknowledging the substantial influence and support of their parents.

Who are Tia and Tamera parents?

Tia and Tamera Mowry, born on 6 July 1978 in Germany, have an intriguing background when it comes to their parents.

Their father, Timothy John Mowry, was born on 4 January 1957 and is of white American descent.

On the other hand, their mother, Darlene Renee Mowry (formerly known as Flowers), was born on 25 October 1956 and has Afro-Bahamian heritage.

As of 2023, Tia and Tamera’s mother, Darlene Mowry, is still alive, dispelling any rumors suggesting otherwise.

There were false speculations about her passing due to her absence from the public eye for five years.

However, she put those rumors to rest by announcing her involvement as a producer in the Hollywood industry.

Currently 66 years old, she continues to contribute her talents and expertise.

The story of Timothy and Darlene began during their high school years in Miami, Florida, where they first crossed paths.

Following their graduation, both of them enlisted in the US Army and eventually achieved the rank of Sergeant.

They tied the knot in 1975 at the young age of 18.

At the time, they were stationed in Germany within the military.

It was during their time in Germany that Tia and Tamera, the twin sisters, were born.

When the girls turned two years old, the Mowry family relocated back to the United States.

They initially settled in Texas before moving to Hawaii, only to return to Texas once again.

In addition to Tia and Tamera, Timothy and Darlene are also parents to two other children.

Tahj, known for his work as an actor and singer, and Tavior, a music producer, complete the Mowry family.

Why Did Tia And Tamera Parents Divorce?

The divorce of Tia and Tamera Mowry’s parents is a well-known fact, but what led to the separation?

According to Tia, it was her father’s infidelity that caused the split.

During an interview with Oprah Winfrey, Tia disclosed that she discovered her father’s affair when she was just a young child.

She vividly remembers coming home from school to find her mother in tears at the kitchen table.

Tia asked her mother what was wrong, and that’s when she learned about her father’s unfaithfulness.

At the time, Tia was only six years old, but the revelation had a significant impact on her.

She admitted that the moment changed her life because, until then, she believed her family was perfect.

However, after that day, things were never the same.

The divorce took a toll on both Tia and Tamera, who were seven and five years old, respectively, when their parents separated.

Tamera shared her perspective on the difficult experience in an interview with Essence magazine.

She expressed how challenging it was for her to witness her parents going through such a tough time.

Even as an adult, it would have been challenging, so as a child, the impact was even greater.

Thankfully, Tia and Tamera had each other for support during this trying period.

They relied on their close bond as sisters to navigate the difficulties that came with growing up in a broken home.

However, through their unwavering sisterly support, they managed to overcome the challenges and maintain a strong bond that continues to this day.

Tia and Tamera parents impact on their career

Both Timothy and Darlene played pivotal roles in paving the way for Tia and Tamera’s achievements.

Darlene Mowry, Tia and Tamera’s mother, initially took the initiative to introduce Tia to the world of print modeling and commercial projects.

Recognizing her daughter’s potential, she went a step further by taking Tia to Los Angeles in hopes of securing her a role in a commercial or a television show.

Darlene was serving in the military at the time, it was not a simple task for her to uproot the family and relocate to pursue their dreams.

She had to make a compelling case in court, advocating for the opportunity to move to Hollywood.

Eventually, the court granted her request, allowing Darlene and the children to make the move, while Timothy had to remain behind.


This significant move enabled the Mowry twins to immerse themselves fully in the entertainment industry, providing them with opportunities to showcase their talents.

Tia and Tamera have graced numerous television shows and films throughout their careers, including notable works such as “Twitches Too,” “Tia & Tamera,” and “Seventeen Again.”

Additionally, during the early 1990s, the twins formed a singing group known as Voices, showcasing their musical abilities.

The unwavering support and determination of both Timothy and Darlene Mowry opened doors for Tia and Tamera, allowing them to flourish in the entertainment world.
